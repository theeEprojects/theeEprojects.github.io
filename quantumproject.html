particle wave duality

Many subatomic particles have more then one pyshical property.

Particle wave duality is the fact that some subatomic particles can be considered as particles while having wave properties.

these wave properties of proven through the diffraction.

These slides and videos shows particle and wave relationships. When the waves release out of a slit, it expands over a large area of space. This is called diffraction. Some kinds of diffraction like sound wave diffraction can diffract(slip throught then come together) around certain objects. The length of its waves gives it this capablility. All objects have this capablity because every particle has wavelength. But the length of the wavelength is different stopping large objects from diffracting. Usually, only subatomic particles diffract.

The photoelectric affect is applied with this rule. the photoelectric effect is an effect discovered in the 1800s that proves that the momentum in light particles have the capablity to knock out electrons from certain metals creating a electric current.
